How it works

We connect directly to the Stockfish WASM engine in your browser so every move is instant. Difficulty controls adjust the Skill Level option (0–20) alongside thoughtful engine timing defaults.

  • Legal move validation powered by chess.js keeps play fair.
  • Smart move highlighting, sounds, and undo support accessible training.
  • Download your games instantly via PGN or FEN export.

Accessibility & UX

Keyboard focus states, ARIA live announcements for check and mate, and high-contrast controls ensure everyone can play comfortably.

Guided lessons with playable diagrams

Six foundational lessons walk you through essential concepts. Each diagram is interactive—drag through ideas, reset, and follow the highlighted arrows.

Transition to Strategy
